CHARGES AND FEES POLICY

Section 1: Product Pricing
Product prices are as listed on the website at the time of order placement.

Prices may change without prior notice, but orders already placed are honored at the original price.

All prices are listed in USD (or local currency) and include applicable platform fees.

Price matching is available for identical products sold by authorized retailers within 7 days of purchase.

Section 2: Shipping and Delivery Charges
Standard shipping: $5.99 for orders under $25, FREE for orders $25 and above.

Express shipping surcharge: Additional $10-15 depending on delivery location and speed.

Same-day delivery: Additional $12.99 for eligible locations.

Remote area delivery: Additional $5-8 surcharge for rural or hard-to-reach locations.

International shipping: Calculated based on weight, dimensions, and destination country.

Section 3: Tax and Regulatory Fees
Sales tax is calculated based on the delivery address and applicable local tax rates.

Tax amounts are clearly displayed during checkout before payment confirmation.

Import duties and customs fees for international orders are the responsibility of the customer unless otherwise stated.

Environmental or recycling fees may apply to certain electronics and are disclosed at checkout.

Section 4: Payment Processing and Transaction Fees
No additional fees for credit card, debit card, or digital wallet payments.

Cash on Delivery (COD): Additional $2 processing fee per order.

Installment payments (Buy Now, Pay Later): May include processing fees depending on the payment partner; terms disclosed before selection.

Currency conversion fees may apply for international orders and are charged by the payment processor, not by the company.

Section 5: Additional Service Fees
Gift wrapping: $3.99 per item.

Extended warranty or protection plans: Priced separately based on product category and coverage level.

Installation or assembly services: Fees vary by product type and complexity.

Premium customer support (priority phone or chat): Free for all customers; no hidden fees.

Section 6: Transparency and Hidden Charges
The company is committed to transparency; all charges are clearly itemized at checkout.

No hidden fees are added after order confirmation.

Order summary displays:
- Subtotal (product prices)
- Shipping charges
- Tax
- Any additional fees (COD, remote delivery, etc.)
- Total amount due

If extra charges appear that were not disclosed at checkout, customers are entitled to a full refund of those charges.

Section 7: Refunds and Fee Adjustments
If a refund is issued, shipping charges are refunded only if the return is due to company error (defective item, incorrect item, etc.).

COD fees, gift wrapping, and optional service fees are non-refundable.

If a customer is overcharged or charged incorrectly, a refund or credit is issued within 3-5 business days of verification.
