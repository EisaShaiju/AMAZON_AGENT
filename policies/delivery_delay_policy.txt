DELIVERY DELAY POLICY

Section 1: Standard Delivery Timelines
Standard delivery: 5-7 business days from order placement
Express delivery: 2-3 business days from order placement
Same-day delivery: Available for orders placed before 12:00 PM in select locations
International delivery: 10-15 business days depending on destination

Section 2: Delay Notification and Tracking
Customers are notified via email and SMS when delays are anticipated.

Delays of 1-2 days: Customer service team sends proactive notification with updated estimated delivery date.

Delays beyond 48 hours: Customers receive notification of automatic refund eligibility and compensation options.

Tracking information is updated in real-time and accessible through the customer account dashboard.

Section 3: Delay Compensation Structure
Minor delays (1-2 days beyond expected delivery):
- Free standard shipping on the next order
- Apology email with explanation

Major delays (3-6 days beyond expected delivery):
- 5% refund or equivalent store credit
- Priority shipping on next order

Severe delays (7+ days beyond expected delivery):
- Full refund of order amount
- 10% additional credit for future purchases
- Option to receive and keep the product at no charge if it arrives later

Section 4: Exceptions and Exclusions
Delays due to incorrect or incomplete shipping addresses provided by customers are not eligible for compensation.

Force majeure events including natural disasters, pandemics, political unrest, or carrier strikes are excluded from standard delay compensation policies.

Remote or hard-to-reach delivery locations may experience longer delivery times, which are communicated at checkout.

Section 5: Customer Actions
Customers experiencing delays should first check tracking information in their account.

If delay exceeds 48 hours beyond expected delivery, customers can:
1. Request a refund through the online portal
2. Contact customer service for expedited resolution
3. Choose to wait for delivery with compensation

Refund requests related to delays must be submitted within 10 days of the expected delivery date.
