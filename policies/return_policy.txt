RETURN POLICY

Section 1: Return Eligibility Windows
Electronics (smartphones, laptops, tablets, cameras): 14 days from delivery date
Clothing, shoes, and accessories: 30 days from delivery date
Home appliances and furniture: 21 days from delivery date
Books, media, and software: 30 days from delivery date (unopened only)
Perishable items (food, flowers, plants): No returns accepted
Personal care and hygiene products: No returns accepted unless defective

Section 2: Condition Requirements for Returns
All returned items must be in unused condition with original packaging intact.

Items must include all original accessories, manuals, warranty cards, and free gifts received with the product.

Products with signs of use, wear, or damage not caused by defects are subject to partial refund (up to 30% deduction).

For electronics, items must pass functionality inspection upon return. Scratched screens, physical damage, or water damage voids return eligibility.

Section 3: Return Process
Step 1: Initiate return through customer account dashboard or by contacting customer support.

Step 2: Select reason for return (defective, incorrect item, changed mind, etc.)

Step 3: Receive return authorization and prepaid shipping label via email (for eligible returns)

Step 4: Pack item securely with all original contents and attach shipping label.

Step 5: Drop off package at designated carrier location or schedule pickup.

Step 6: Track return shipment through account dashboard.

Step 7: Inspection completed within 3 business days of receiving returned item.

Step 8: Refund processed within 5-7 business days after successful inspection.

Section 4: Return Shipping Costs
Returns due to product defects, damage, or incorrect items: Free return shipping provided by the company.

Returns for change of mind or unwanted items: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs, which may be deducted from the refund amount.

For high-value items (over $500), insured return shipping is recommended and available at customer's expense.

Section 5: Exchange vs Return
Exchanges are available for defective items or incorrect sizes/colors.

Exchanges are prioritized over returns and are processed faster (typically 3-5 business days).

If the desired exchange item is out of stock, a full refund will be issued instead.

Section 6: Special Conditions
Items purchased during special sales, clearance, or promotional events may have modified return policies clearly stated at the time of purchase.

Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless defective or damaged upon arrival.

Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" at checkout are not eligible for returns or exchanges.

Gift returns are accepted with proof of purchase; refund is issued as store credit.
